Refund Policy
Last updated: April 3, 2026

Summary: All WaveStamp subscriptions come with a 7-day free trial. No payment is taken during the trial. After the trial ends, all charges are final and non-refundable. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

Free Trial

Every new Pro and Founding Member subscription begins with a 7-day free trial. During this period:

  • You have full access to all Pro features
  • No payment is collected
  • You can cancel at any time before the trial ends with no charge
  • If you do not cancel, your subscription automatically converts to a paid plan on day 8

The free trial is designed to give you full access to evaluate WaveStamp before committing. We encourage you to test all features — exports, resolution options, and visualizers — during this period.

No Refund Policy

Because WaveStamp offers a free tier and a 7-day free trial before any charge occurs, all subscription payments are non-refundable once the trial period has ended and billing has begun.

This applies to:

  • Monthly Pro subscriptions ($5/month)
  • Founding Member subscriptions ($3/month)
  • Any partial month remaining after cancellation

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account menu → Manage billing. Cancelling is immediate and you will not be charged again. However, you retain full Pro access until the end of your current paid billing period — we do not cut off access early.

For example, if your billing renews on the 15th and you cancel on the 5th, you keep Pro access through the 15th at no additional charge.

Exceptions

We may consider refund requests in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate charges — if you were charged more than once for the same subscription period due to a technical error
  • Charge after cancellation — if you were billed after properly cancelling your subscription
  • Extended service outage — if WaveStamp was completely inaccessible for more than 72 consecutive hours during a paid billing period

To request a review, contact us within 7 days of the charge with your account email and a description of the issue. Refund requests submitted after 7 days of the charge may not be considered.
