Refund Policy
Last updated: April 3, 2026

WaveStamp offers a 7-day free trial on all paid plans so you can try the full product before being charged. Because of this, our general policy is that all sales are final after the trial period ends.

Free Trial

All new Pro and Founding Member subscribers receive a 7-day free trial. You will not be charged during this period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you owe nothing.

No Refunds After Trial

Once your free trial has ended and your first payment has been processed, we do not offer refunds for that billing period. This applies to both monthly charges and any partial month of service.

Exceptions We Will Review

We will review refund requests in the following situations:

  • You were charged after cancelling your subscription due to a technical error
  • You were charged twice for the same billing period
  • You experienced a significant service outage that prevented use of the product for the majority of your billing period

To request a review, contact us within 7 days of the charge in question.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account menu. After cancellation, you retain full Pro access through the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after that.

Founding Member Pricing

The Founding Member rate ($3/month) is locked in for as long as your subscription remains continuously active. If you cancel and resubscribe, the Founding Member rate is not guaranteed to be available.

How to Request a Review

Email [email protected] with your account email and a description of the issue. We respond within 2 business days.
