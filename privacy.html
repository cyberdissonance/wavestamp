Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 3, 2026

WaveStamp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your email address when you create an account, and your name/profile photo if you sign in with Google.
  • Usage data — how many exports you've used in a given month, your subscription status, and your account type (free or Pro).
  • Payment information — we do not store your credit card details. All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We receive a customer ID and subscription status from Stripe after a successful payment.
  • Local data — your project settings, style preferences, and lyric data are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your WaveStamp account
  • To process payments and manage your subscription through Stripe
  • To enforce usage limits on the free tier (export counts)
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, subscription receipts) — we do not send marketing emails without your consent
  • To improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate WaveStamp:

  • Supabase — stores your account data (email, subscription status, export count) in a secure PostgreSQL database. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Stripe — processes all payments. Your card details are entered directly on Stripe's servers and are never transmitted to or stored by WaveStamp. Stripe Privacy Policy →
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in method. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile photo from Google. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Vercel — hosts the WaveStamp application. Vercel may log basic request data (IP address, user agent) for security purposes. Vercel Privacy Policy →

4. Cookies and Local Storage

WaveStamp uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store:

  • Your project settings and style preferences
  • Your session token (managed by Supabase Auth)
  • Your monthly export count (for free tier tracking)

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except where explicitly noted (session token for authentication). You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

5. Audio and Image Files

All audio files and images you upload to WaveStamp are processed entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers. Your music and artwork remain 100% private on your device.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription or delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days. Export count data and subscription history may be retained for up to 90 days for billing reconciliation purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

WaveStamp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of WaveStamp after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
