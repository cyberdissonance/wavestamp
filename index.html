Auto-timestamp

Go to the Lyrics tab, paste all your lyric lines (no timestamps needed), upload your audio, then click "Auto-timestamp all lines". The engine analyses the audio's energy and distributes each lyric line automatically. Fine-tune any line by editing its timestamp directly.

Lyric format

Manual format for bulk import:

0:00 Yeah...
0:03 They said patience pays
0:19 Look, I been grindin'

Export MP4

Click "Export MP4" in the top bar. Choose your resolution (720p or 1080p), progress bar visibility, then hit Render. The video encodes at full speed — no need to play through. File downloads automatically when done. Works best in Chrome or Edge.

Platform aspect ratios

Use 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok & Instagram Reels, 1:1 for Instagram posts.
